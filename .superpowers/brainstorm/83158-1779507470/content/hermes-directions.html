Runway Minimal

Most fashion-forward and sparse. It feels luxurious, but pushes hardest away from conventional storefront behavior.

Pros

  • Most striking visual identity
  • Strong brand-art direction feel
  • Excellent for premium storytelling

Cons

  • Weakest direct shopping cues
  • Requires more UX discipline later

Recommendation

I recommend Direction A: Editorial Gallery if the goal is to echo Hermès most closely while still building a reusable system for headers, typography, buttons, and product cards. It gives us the strongest luxury tone without making the shopping flow feel too abstract.
